# Smart Civic Engagement Desk User Stories

This document captures handoff-ready user stories for the Smart Civic Engagement Desk wireframe portal.

## Scope

The portal supports three primary audiences:

- Citizens who participate in civic programs and track loyalty rewards.
- Local government agents who assist citizens across call center, email, and walk-in service channels.
- Managers who review service feedback and training opportunities.

## Citizen Stories

### CS-01 View loyalty status

As a citizen, I want to view my loyalty points and current tier so I understand my standing in community programs.

Acceptance notes:

- The dashboard shows current point balance.
- The dashboard shows current status or tier.
- The dashboard makes milestone progress visible.

### CS-02 Review engagement history

As a citizen, I want to review my engagement history so I can see how past actions contributed to points and milestones.

Acceptance notes:

- The dashboard shows recent engagement items.
- The detailed history page shows timeline entries across multiple channels.
- Engagement entries include dates, action types, and points earned.

### CS-03 Receive personalized recommendations

As a citizen, I want personalized recommendations so I know which next activity best matches my interests.

Acceptance notes:

- The dashboard highlights recommended next-best actions.
- Recommendations reference recent interests or prior participation.
- Recommendations show the value of taking the next step, such as additional points or milestone progress.

### CS-04 Rate service experience

As a citizen, I want to rate my recent interaction with an agent so the agency can improve service quality.

Acceptance notes:

- The citizen view indicates that service ratings can be submitted.
- Feedback contributes to a management-facing analytics view.
- The wireframe makes the connection between service rating and continuous improvement clear.

## Agent Stories

### AG-01 See citizen context at contact start

As an agent, I want to see a citizen's loyalty balance, tier, and recent activity when they contact us so I can personalize support immediately.

Acceptance notes:

- The agent desk shows a selected citizen profile.
- The selected profile includes loyalty points, tier, and recent engagement history.
- The profile can be updated by selecting another citizen in the queue.

### AG-02 Recommend next-best actions

As an agent, I want suggested next-best actions so I can recommend programs likely to increase participation.

Acceptance notes:

- The agent desk shows a personalized recommendation for the selected citizen.
- The programs page displays suggested enrollment options.
- Recommendations align to engagement history or milestone progress.

### AG-03 Resolve issues with prior context

As an agent, I want access to recent engagement and service context so I can resolve citizen issues efficiently without repeating questions.

Acceptance notes:

- The agent desk exposes recent activity and interaction context.
- The wireframe indicates how prior participation supports faster service.
- The design supports service across walk-in, phone, or email interactions.

### AG-04 Recognize loyalty milestones

As an agent, I want to recognize citizen milestones during service so the citizen feels acknowledged and encouraged.

Acceptance notes:

- The selected citizen view shows current milestone progress.
- The interface includes recognition or appreciation prompts.
- The wireframe ties milestone recognition to suggested outreach language.

### AG-05 Access knowledge guidance in workflow

As an agent, I want knowledge management details in the workflow so I can handle enrollment, policy, and escalation steps without leaving the desk view.

Acceptance notes:

- The agent desk includes knowledge guidance tied to the selected citizen.
- The knowledge center contains scripts, policy notes, and escalation prompts.
- The interface supports quick navigation between service context and knowledge content.

## Manager Stories

### MG-01 Review citizen feedback analytics

As a manager, I want citizen feedback analytics so I can identify coaching needs and recognize high-performing staff.

Acceptance notes:

- The analytics view shows an overall service score.
- The analytics view includes trends or rating dimensions.
- The analytics view highlights coaching and recognition opportunities.

### MG-02 Connect feedback to service improvement

As a manager, I want to connect service ratings with engagement context so I can improve both service delivery and advocacy outcomes.

Acceptance notes:

- The analytics view references coaching focus areas.
- Feedback patterns can be interpreted alongside engagement behavior.
- The wireframe demonstrates how citizen sentiment informs future training or escalation planning.

## Handoff Notes

- These stories are aligned to the current static wireframe and can be expanded into epics, features, or backlog items later.
- Acceptance notes describe intended behavior for the prototype and can be converted into formal acceptance criteria during implementation planning.
