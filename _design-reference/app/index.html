Demo tips

  • Open Agent Desk first — Aisha Patel (Gold) is auto-loaded.
  • Try Quick pick to switch citizens (Maria=Bronze near Silver, Robert=Champion).
  • Click Enroll on a recommendation to watch points/tier update live, then visit the Citizen view.
  • Type "pothole" or "recycling" in the KB search.
  • Optional AI: open DevTools console and run SCED_OPENAI_KEY="sk-..." then click Refresh on recommendations.
