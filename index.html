Solution Flow

From participation to personalized service

  1. Citizens participate in local programs, give feedback, volunteer, or attend events.
  2. Each action creates engagement history and adds loyalty points to the citizen profile.
  3. Agents access the profile in real time during service interactions.
  4. The portal suggests next-best actions, milestone recognition, issue resolution cues, and relevant knowledge content.
